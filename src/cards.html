Cards

The are two categories of cards in the game the first being industry cards, industry cards once played remain on the board and contribute to emissions each turn. They continue to contribute to emissions until destroyed. The second type of card is social, social cards these have one time effect and once played don't stay on the board. An example of this effect would be to destroy one of the opponents industry cards.
