What is X DAYS LEFT?

In X Days Left, you play as a mad scientist, Doctor Y. After years of trying to protect the environment you finally reach your boiling point and realise the only way to tackle climate change is to attack the issue head on. After leaving your job as a respected climate change researcher you start a movement, going all round the world tackling influential leaders whose failure to address these issues is running the world to its last breath.
