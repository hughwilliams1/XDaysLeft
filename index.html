Welcome to X Days Left

X Days left follows the story a mad scientist, Doctor Y. After years of research and testing you discover the planet is on the brink of destruction due to global warming. When the scientific community refuse to listen to your plea you reach your boiling point, deciding direct action is the only way to save the planet. Will you be successful in your journey and force the leaders of world to act, or is it already too late?
